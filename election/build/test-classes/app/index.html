Quiz for Electoral Candidates

  1. Do you believe is the main mission of EU and how will you spread that mission to your fellow Citizens?

  2. Will you increase the awareness of the significance of your position to a wide range of Citizens?

  3. Do you think your party will strike a balance between protecting the ecological integrity of Finland’s forests and the need for an economically and environmentally sustainable forestry sector?

  4. Do you think your party stands a chance in creating a national pool to fund social and environmental programs from oil revenue?

  5. Do you think your party will ensure that Finland’s cities are resilient and sustainable?

  6. Do religion influence the outcome of an election and does it have a bearing in the economic outlook of a country?

  7. Should normal democratic engagement and posturing be expected to work in the face of extreme democratic outcomes?

  8. Have social media become an effective tool to gauge the performance of products, including that in politics?

  9. Do you think the ambit of political branding spread beyond the realms of an election?

  10. If your party forms the next government, will you foster greater levels of cooperation on environmental issue among First Nations, industry, non-profits, NGOs, charities and various provincial and municipal governments?
